Welcome to the investment evaluation station where nothing is financial advice (or any advice at all)

What is this?

This is a crude FOMO prevention tool.

But why?

You may have overheard a coworker being able to afford the downpayment for their dream home by cashing out their Tesla stocks. Or perhaps you stumbled upon an influencer peddling the latest crypto tokens with a promise to "moon". Being exposed to so much positive news about capital gain could make your self-investing senses tingling. Next thing you know, you are ready to put your hard earned money to work for you by setting up a self-direct trading account.


Use the form below to see if the risk is worth the reward.

The assumptions:

The bigger your order size is, the higher your risk will become

The higher your expected return is, the longer your market exposure becomes (and in turn, the higher risk you take)
